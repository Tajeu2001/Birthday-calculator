Akan Name Calculator

What the application does

The Akan Names calculator is a very fun app where anyone can find out which is the exact day they were born! To get to know this powerful information one has to fill in a form that requires the birth date and year,plus the gender.When the user clicks the submit button,they get to find out their born day of the week in a very fun way.The user is given a Ghanian cultural name,called "Akan name" according to the day of the week they were born.
